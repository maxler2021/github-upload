DAVID BOWIE

About

David Bowie is a musical artist whose body of work spans decades and transcends genres and conventions. His debut album “David Bowie” was released in 1967, but his breakout hit came in 1969 with the single Space Oddity, which reached the Top 5 in the UK. His signature glam rock sound and image premiered with his best loved album The Rise and Fall of Ziggy Stardust and the Spiders from Mars in 1972. He went on to release a total of 25 studio albums during the course of his career. Bowie received many accolades, over the years, including the Grammy Lifetime Achievement Award in 2006.
